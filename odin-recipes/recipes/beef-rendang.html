Beef Rendang

Recipe from All Recipes by John Mitzewich, converted to Indonesian Measurements!

Prep Time: 25 minutes

Cook Time: 4 hrs 5 mins

Total Time: 4 hrs 30 min

Servings: 8

Yield: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef chuck cut into 5cm pieces
  • 4 shallots, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 4 cm piece ginger, peeles and sliced
  • 4 cm galangal, peeled and sliced
  • 2 serrano chile peppers - stemmed, seeded, and sliced
  • 1 Fresno chili pepper, stemmed, seeded, and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon Korean red pepper flakes
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 0.125 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 0.125 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 talespoons vegetable oil
  • 400 ml full-fat coconut milk
  • 2 teaspoons of tamarind paste
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 0.5 stalk lemonmgrass (white part)

Directions

  1. Cut the beef into 5 cm cubes
  2. In a food processor or blender, combine:
    • shallots
    • garlic
    • ginger
    • galangal
    • serrano chilies
    • Fresno chili
    • salt
    • red pepper flakes
    • coriander
    • turmeric
    • cardamom
    • nutmeg
    Blend until it forms a very fine paste
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the curry paste and cook while stirring until it begins to dry slightly
  4. Add the beef and stir to coat with the paste
  5. Add:
    • coconut milk
    • tamarind paste
    • brown sugar
    Stir to combine.
